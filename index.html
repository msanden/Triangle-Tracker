    Application guidelines:
  • An equilateral triangle has all equal lengths,
  • An isosceles triangle has two equal lengths,
  • A scalene has no equal lengths but the sum of any two sides is greater than the third side,
  • A triangle cannot be formed if the sum of any two sides is equal to or less than the third remaining side. For example, the values 9, 4 & 3 cannot form a triangle.
