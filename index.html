    Triangle tracker program rules:
  • All sides must be equal in an equilateral triangle.
  • Two sides are exactly equal in an isosceles triangle.
  • None of the sides are equal in a scalene triangle. However, the sum of any two sides of the triangle must be greater than the third side.
  • Most importantly, if the sum of any two sides of the triangle is equal to or less than the third remaining side, then a triangle CANNOT be formed using those values. (For example, the values 9, 4 & 3 cannot form a triangle.)
